 ________________________________________________________________________________

                            Consensus model scoring                            
 ________________________________________________________________________________


 Site scoring, i.e. the calculation of SFOM values, is based on three contributions:
 Consensus rate, mean consensus distance and site occupancy. The SFOM value is then
 taken to judge the reliability of the site.
 Fundamentally, all consensus sites are written to the output files. The reliability
 classification is (only) a decision help for the manual truncation of the output.
 SFOM values calculated by following model:

 criterion             | weight |                         FOM defined as        
 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 consensus rate        |  1.00  | group-weighted mean of n(agreements,group) / n(solutions,group)
 overall mean distance |  0.50  |                        1 / 1 + d(mean)        
 source-site occupancy |  0.00  |                 occupancy (peak height) value 
 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 Site reliability color scheme:

 predicted reliablity   |  indicator                  
 ------------------------------------                 
 high   (SFOM  >  0.65) |   ##  ##                    
 medium (SFOM  >  0.35) |   ++  ++                    
 low    (SFOM  <= 0.35) |   --  --   		 
 ------------------------------------
